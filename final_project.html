Here are a few places that you should definitely go to when visiting Cape Town:
  • Kirstenbosh - Exploring and being one with nature, take a stroll or hike in a botanical garden.
  • Waterfront - In the heart of Cape Town, visit and experience beautiful scenaries with state of the art restaurants and activities.
  • Constantia - Beautiful scenary of table mountain and the beach within a well known residential area.
  • Muizenberg - For beach activities, swimming, surfing, and whale watching.
  • Stellenbosh - For the best wine testing experience, wine farms and farmers markets.
